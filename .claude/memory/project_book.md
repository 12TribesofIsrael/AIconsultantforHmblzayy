## What this book is (pivoted Apr 21)

**Thomas's weight-loss memoir.** Zay named it on the Apr 19 rest-day stream: *"Yo, AI Bible Gospel, I want you to write a book — document how you lost your weight. That's gonna be powerful for the people."* Thomas said *"say less."*

This is NOT the three-pillar "two parallel journeys" book in the earlier draft of `outline.md` — that framing was written before the shoutout was paraphrased and Zay's specific ask was clarified. **Thomas's decision Apr 21:** run with Zay's ask. The Faith Walk is the catalyzing backdrop and the inspiration, not a co-equal subject.

## Core premise

Thomas has struggled with weight **all his life** (lose-gain cycles, multiple attempts). Watching Zay walk 3,000 miles on faith put "pedal to the metal" — turned a lifelong struggle into a locked-in journey with a real goal. The book is written for people who have been in the same loop Thomas was in.

## The shoutout — core chapter beat (was Ch 11, now anchor chapter)

The moment Zay named the book contains a **misread**: Thomas had been talking in chat about "a play I'm running right now that I never thought was possible, until I've seen you walk almost 400 miles." The "play" Thomas actually meant was the **Anointed Bible Video Maker** (see `user_anointed_bible_video_maker.md`). Zay heard "play" through the weight thread Thomas had been planting in chat for weeks and through the prior Town Hall where Zay had credited Thomas for the Faith Walk tracker — and named the weight-loss book. Thomas said *"say less"* and accepted the redirect.

Full paraphrase lives at `../faithwalkbook/personal/shoutout/paraphrase.md`.

## Audience & positioning (decided)

- **Audience:** people struggling with weight (often faith-adjacent, Zay's community + broader). Not consultants. Not HMBL brand fans specifically. Not faith community broadly.
- **Zay's read on the book's value:** "powerful for the people" trying to lose weight — Thomas echoing this.
- **Thomas as writer:** not a certified trainer / nutritionist / doctor. He's the guy in the chat who showed up, got inspired, did the work. Memoir voice, not how-to voice.

## Implications for existing scaffolding

- **`../faithwalkbook/outline.md`** — needs rewrite. 14-chapter 4-act structure around Zay's walk is now mis-shaped. New structure is a memoir arc: lifelong weight pattern → Zay inspiration → the daily discipline → the shoutout → the ongoing finish.
- **Ch 11 (the shoutout) remains the pivot chapter** but is now the climax of a weight-loss arc, not the "the-book-becomes-self-aware" chapter.

- **Appendices A (deliverables ledger) and B (walk day-by-day)** — still useful, but they're appendix material now, not co-equal narrative.
- **The Anointed Bible Video Maker** (Thomas's actual "play") does NOT live in this book. It lives on `aibiblegospels.com`. Book mentions it only in Ch 11 to explain the misread.

## How to apply

- When drafting any chapter, frame from Thomas's weight-journey POV. Zay is the catalyst, not the subject.
- Faith Walk tools Thomas shipped (tracker, overlay, etc.) stay in the story as *discipline proof* — showing up for Zay became the muscle that reshaped Thomas's body. They don't get their own chapters.
- When describing Zay, respect that the book's readers may not know HMBL. Introduce Zay through Thomas's eyes, not as a brand bio.
- **Do NOT treat this book as a how-to-lose-weight guide.** No workout plans, no meal diagrams. It's a story of what changed.
