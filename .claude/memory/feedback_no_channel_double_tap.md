After sending an outreach message on one channel (IG DM, text, email), wait at least 24-48h before sending on a second channel. Don't double-tap within minutes/hours.

**Why:** Double-tapping two channels back-to-back flips the read from "gift delivered" to "please respond" — it reads thirsty and undercuts any "no strings attached" framing the first message established. Hit on Apr 22: user sent IG DM to Zay, then asked whether to also send text immediately; answer was hold, minimum 24-48h, then text becomes a clean second touch with a legit excuse ("wasn't sure IG was the right spot").

**How to apply:** When recommending outreach sequencing, default to one channel per 24-48h. Read receipts being off (or any ambiguity about whether the first message was seen) doesn't change this — that's normal for people with big followings, not a signal to pile on. Also: don't treat real-time stream body language ("he's on his phone texting a lot") as a response signal — it could be anyone. Let the first message breathe.
