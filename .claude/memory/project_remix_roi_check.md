Apr 25, 2026 — Ran the 4-agent ROI check (Reddit, YouTube, X, case-study blogs) on the IG Reels remix funnel for @AIBibleGospels driving traffic to faithwalklive.com.

**Verdict: REFRAME.** Re-uploading another creator's vertical clip (e.g. Philly FAME) with text overlay is the WORST of available options. 3 of 4 agents said native Remix split-screen wins; Reddit dissented saying the new Aug 2025 native **Repost button** beats both Remix and re-upload, and that Remix split-screen is a "niche reaction format."

**The bigger finding all 4 agreed on:** the format isn't the real lever — the CTA is. Link-in-bio CTR is brutally low. Hard Reddit data point: u/calligraphy_snowy reported 500K Reel views → 20 bio-link clicks → 0 sales = **0.004% CTR**. Reels are reach plays; **Story link stickers are the actual click path** (one tap, no bio detour). The "Comment WALK → ManyChat auto-DM" play reportedly delivers 3-5x bio-link clicks but requires setup user has deferred.

**Recommended funnel for any remix going forward:**
1. Native Repost (1 tap) on the original creator's Reel → if unavailable, native Remix split-screen
2. **IMMEDIATE Story with link sticker** to faithwalklive.com (this is the click-driver, not the Reel)
3. Bio link as backstop only

**Hard rules confirmed:**
- IG enforced **5-hashtag cap** Dec 18, 2025 — anything over wasted
- @mention in **first 125 chars** of caption
- Text overlay: **top third or upper-middle** — bottom 280px is UI-occluded
- Original audio is fine (ThatRandomAgency 2025 A/B: no significant difference vs trending)
- Watermarks from other platforms = Meta deprioritization (Buffer + Later + Mosseri quote on-record)
- Sub-10K-account engagement rate ceiling: **6.23%** (Sprout Social) — set realistic expectations with Zay

**Why:** all 4 reports converged on this; user (Thomas) was mid-edit on the wrong path (re-upload of Philly FAME clip with text overlay) and would have shipped a reach-penalized Reel pointing to a CTA that converts at <0.01%.

**How to apply:** When Thomas asks about Reels distribution or remix tactics, default to Repost-first → Remix-fallback → Story-sticker-as-click-path. Don't recommend the re-upload-with-overlay path even if it looks "more polished" — it underperforms. Don't push ManyChat unless he asks (deferred Apr 25 due to overwhelm; can revisit when Reels cadence is steady).
