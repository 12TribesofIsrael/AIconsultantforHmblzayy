# Minister Zay — Press Coverage & Interviews

## 1. Voyage ATL — "Inspiring Conversations with Minister Zay"
- **URL:** https://voyageatl.com/interview/inspiring-conversations-with-minister-zay-of-stay-humble-stay-hungry-clothing/
- **Key Details:**
  - Grew up in Nicetown, North Philadelphia
  - Incarcerated at age 14 on a gun charge
  - Brand launched Spring 2017
  - Started by selling candy in high school, then water outside the Art Museum
  - Sold merch from his car trunk before opening stores
  - Celebrities wearing HMBL: Moneybagg Yo, Queen Naija, A Boogie With Da Hoodie, Asian Doll
  - Received CBS award and recognition from Philadelphia's mayor
  - Won "Best Small Business" Award
  - Organizing "HMBL World" tour launching in Atlanta
  - Quote: "I wasn't afraid to show people when I was at the bottom. My audience saw me come up"
