## Why it works (so future variants don't break the formula)

- **Self-routes the audience.** "On Discord ➡️ X — Not on Discord ➡️ Y" lets every viewer pick their lane without the poster choosing for them. No one feels excluded.
- **Discord first respects Zay's house.** HMBL Discord is Zay's owned community; faithwalklive.com is a supporter-built surface. Order communicates priorities correctly.
- **Reverent register, not hype.** No `🔥`, no all-caps, no `!!!`. Chat is already screaming hype during a milestone — a calmer single-line message stands out *more*, not less. Particularly important during sensitive arcs (post-injury, hospital, etc.).
- **Single line, no `https://`.** Twitch chat auto-links bare domains. Multi-line gets scrolled past.
- **Specific number.** "41 miles" lands harder than "miles." If you don't know the number for sure, drop the count and say "back on the road."

## When to post

- **At end of stream**, after Zay confirms he's stopping (eating, hotel, etc.) — the chat is at peak engagement and not actively narrating Zay's movement
- Repost every ~10-15 min as new viewers join the chat scroll
- Skip during danger / accident / mid-walk drama — read the room

## Hard rules

- **Never post mid-walk** while Zay is actively walking and the title says "X miles to {city}". Per `feedback_tracker_timing.md` and `feedback_public_copy_safety.md` — public copy never narrates Zay's real-time location.
- **Never modify the Discord URL.** `discord.gg/MzWAdRbDqu` is the permanent HMBL University invite; any other URL routes wrong.
- **`faithwalklive.com` only — no path or UTM in chat.** UTMs work in browser bar but read as spam in chat.
