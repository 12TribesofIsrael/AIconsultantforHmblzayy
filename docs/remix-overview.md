**Goal:** Drive external traffic from social video to `faithwalklive.com` (the tracker), since Zay's team isn't promoting it themselves.

**Created:** 2026-04-24
**Execution window:** Starts tomorrow evening (after work)
**Source of recommendations:** Parallel research across Reddit, YouTube, X, and case-study blogs (HubSpot, Buffer, Rival IQ, Socialinsider, Creator Science). All numbers below are from published benchmarks, not vibes.

---

## The Strategy in One Sentence

Use the **remix method** to grow @AIBibleGospels followers off larger creators' audiences (top of funnel), then use **IG Story Link Stickers + DM-automation on Reels** to convert those followers into tracker visits (bottom of funnel).

---

## The Two-Stage Model

| Stage | Tactic | Job | Metric |
|-------|--------|-----|--------|
| **Top of funnel** | Remix method (Stitch / Duet / Remix / Quote) | Grow @AIBibleGospels followers off larger audiences | New followers, profile visits, saves |
| **Bottom of funnel** | IG Story Link Sticker + ManyChat DM flow + UTM'd bio link | Convert followers to tracker clicks | Tracker visits per UTM source |

**You need both.** Remixing alone grows an audience that never clicks. Click plays alone convert a static audience. They're sequential, not competing.

## The 3 ROI-Ranked Click Tactics

| Rank | Tactic | Published CTR | Setup |
|------|--------|---------------|-------|
| **1** | **IG Reels DM-automation** — "comment WALK, I'll DM you the tracker" via ManyChat/Inro | **12–18%** (5–9× bio link) | ~$15/mo, ~1 hour setup |
| **2** | **IG Story Link Sticker** — daily, mid-story, custom CTA + poll pairing | **0.5–3%** (realistic 1–2%) | Free, 2 min/day |
| **3** | **X: video in body, link in first reply** — pinned tweet is the one in-body-link exception | Avoids 30–50% reach penalty (Buffer 18.8M-post study, Mar 2025) | Free |

Supporting: TikTok text overlay at 0.5s = 40–50% higher CTR. Pinned top comment on TikTok = ~31% engagement lift (softer number, still worth it).

## The Meta-Move — UTM Scheme (Non-Optional)

Tag every outbound link BEFORE rolling anything out. Without this you're flying blind.

**Format:** `https://faithwalklive.com/?utm_source=<channel>&utm_medium=<surface>&utm_campaign=faithwalk-day-N`

| Channel | `utm_source` | `utm_medium` |
|---------|--------------|--------------|
| IG Story sticker | `ig_story` | `sticker` |
| IG Reels DM (ManyChat) | `ig_reels` | `dm` |
| IG bio link | `ig_bio` | `bio` |
| TikTok bio | `tiktok` | `bio` |
| TikTok pinned comment | `tiktok` | `pinned_comment` |
| X pinned tweet | `x` | `pinned` |
| X reply link | `x` | `reply` |
| YouTube Shorts description | `yt_shorts` | `description` |

Update `utm_campaign=faithwalk-day-N` where N = current walk day. Lets you see which tactics drove clicks on which day.

## Implementation Blueprint — Tomorrow Evening

Work through these in order. Each block is scoped to ~20–30 minutes.

### Block 1 — UTM infrastructure (30 min, free)
1. Open a new Google Sheet called "faithwalklive-utm-log."
2. Columns: Date | Day # | Channel | utm_source | utm_medium | utm_campaign | Full URL | Clicks (fill later).
3. Pre-generate the 8 URLs from the table above for Day 30 (or whatever day tomorrow is).
4. Update IG bio link to the `utm_source=ig_bio` tagged URL.
5. Update TikTok bio link to `utm_source=tiktok` tagged URL.
6. Update X pinned-tweet link to `utm_source=x&utm_medium=pinned` tagged URL.

### Block 2 — First IG Story Link Sticker (15 min, free)
1. Pick one Twitch clip from today's walk (pull from `twitch.tv/hmblzayy/clips`).
2. Record a 7-sec reaction/commentary Story over it.
3. Add Link Sticker with custom text "See today's checkpoint" → paste UTM'd Story URL.
4. Place sticker mid-story (not end).
5. Add a quiz sticker: "Where do you think he'll be tomorrow?" with 2–3 city options.
6. Post.

### Block 3 — ManyChat DM flow for Reels (60 min, ~$15/mo)
1. Sign up at manychat.com (Instagram plan).
2. Connect @AIBibleGospels IG account.
3. Create new Flow: "Walk Tracker Keyword."
4. Trigger: comment keyword "WALK" on any Reel.
5. Auto-reply DM body: "Live map + daily miles → [UTM'd URL]. Following the whole Walk daily at @AIBibleGospels."
6. Test with your own comment before going live.

### Block 4 — First Reel with DM-automation CTA (20 min, free once flow exists)
1. Stitch one of Zay's recent Twitch clips (or a 15-sec walk moment).
2. On-screen text at 0.5s: "Zay just hit Day N. Comment WALK for the live tracker."
3. Voiceover same CTA at the end.
4. Post. Pin "comment WALK" as top comment from your own account.
5. Reply to every "WALK" comment for the first 2 hours (ManyChat auto-DMs, but manual thank-you reply triggers algorithm).

### Block 5 — First remix (30 min, free)
1. Pick ONE creator from the top 5 list — start with Chrisean Rock or Zay himself (easiest warm-up).
2. Find their highest-engagement post from the last 48 hours.
3. Stitch/Remix/Quote it with your angle + tracker overlay.
4. Triple-tag them (caption + on-screen + voiceover).
5. Post within their audience's peak window.
6. Within 60 min, leave a thoughtful (non-promotional) comment on their original post.

**Total time:** ~2.5 hours. Covers infrastructure + first live execution on all three ROI tactics + first remix.

## What NOT to Waste Time On

Flagged as hype-without-data in the research:

- **QR codes on vertical video** — no credible scan-rate data. Viewers are holding the phone they'd scan with.
- **Remixes as direct click drivers** — no case studies exist. Remixes grow followers, not clicks. Don't measure them on tracker CTR.
- **"Proven CTA voiceover scripts"** — vibes, no A/B data published anywhere.
- **YouTube Shorts pinned-comment links** — YouTube disabled clickable links in Shorts pinned comments August 2023. Shorts are a discovery channel only; the click path is Shorts → channel → description.

## Strategic Context

Direct-to-site is not the highest-ROI structure. Matt Gray ($13.8M creator funnel) and Pat Flynn (3.5B views) both run short-form → DM/email capture → tracker + daily recap email. You already HAVE the daily recap content (the walk itself). The DM-automation flow is the capture mechanism that completes the loop.

The remix method feeds the top of that funnel. The Story sticker + DM flow harvests it. They're sequential jobs, not competing plays.
