## The 3 daily plays (~12 min total)

### PLAY 1 — TikTok Stitch on Zay's 2M-view post (4 min)

**Why this and not Repost:** Stitch puts you in the discover graph for everyone who watched Zay's original. Repost just stamps your username on his video. Stitch is the only evidence-backed audience-borrow mechanic (Khaby Lame precedent + Brendan Kane / Modern Millie 3-10x reach data on small accounts).

1. Open TT → search `@hmblzayy` → tap his pinned 2M-view "Yeshua the Messiah" video (URL above)
2. Tap the **paper-airplane icon** (right side, third icon)
3. Tap **Stitch**
4. **Drag the trim handles** to clip 5 seconds — pick the most curious frame of his footage (lead with HIS hook, not your face — Jenny Hoyos's tested rule)
5. Tap **Next →**
6. **Record 7-10 sec** holding the red button. Read the day's verse + script (verses are pre-extracted below — do NOT re-quote from memory):

   ```
   Day [N]. He's [miles] miles in.
   "[verse]"
   Live tracker → faithwalklive.com 🙏🏾
   ```

7. Tap **Next →** → caption:

   ```
   Day [N]. [miles] miles. He's still walking. 🙏🏾
   Live map → faithwalklive.com
   #FaithWalk #MinisterZay #Yeshua
   ```

8. Tap **Post**
9. **Pin the link comment IMMEDIATELY:** scroll to your own comments section → comment:

   ```
   Live tracker → https://faithwalklive.com/?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=stitch&utm_campaign=faithwalk-day-N
   ```

   Then long-press your own comment → **Pin**

**Fallback:** If yesterday's Stitch on the 2M post got <50 views, swap today's target to whichever of Zay's last-48h posts has the highest views (`tiktok.com/@hmblzayy` → top of feed). Use the same script.

### PLAY 3 — Instagram Story link sticker (5 min) — KEPT from v2.10.0

This is the actual click engine. Story link sticker CTR is **2.5-4%** vs pinned comment CTR **0.4-1.2%** (5-10x difference per the case-study agent). **Do not skip.**

1. IG → tap **profile pic top-left with `+`** → **Add to Story**
2. Background: screenshot or 5-sec screen-record of [faithwalklive.com](https://faithwalklive.com) → choose from camera roll
3. Tap **Sticker icon** (smiley square top) → tap **Link**
4. Paste the URL with day-rotating UTM:

   ```
   https://faithwalklive.com/?utm_source=ig_story&utm_medium=sticker&utm_campaign=faithwalk-day-N
   ```

5. Tap **Done** → in **Customize sticker**, type: `Watch the route →` → **Done**
6. **Drag link sticker to MIDDLE of screen** (not top, not bottom — IG UI covers both)
7. Tap **Sticker icon** again → **Poll** → `Will he reach California?` / `He's locked in 🔥` / `Praying him there 🙏🏾`
8. Place poll **below** link sticker
9. Tap **Aa** (text tool) at top → type:

   ```
   Day [N]. [miles] mi to [destination].
   He's still walking. 🙏🏾
   ```

10. Place text at **top** of screen
11. Tap **Your Story** bottom-left
