## TODAY — Post #2: The Story (click play — DO NOT SKIP)

This is the actual click-driver. The Reel gets reach. The Story gets clicks. Do this within 30 minutes of posting the Reel.

1. Open Instagram
2. Tap your **profile picture (top-left)** with the `+` symbol → **Add to Story**

```
https://faithwalklive.com/?utm_source=ig_story&utm_medium=sticker&utm_campaign=faithwalk-day-30
```
